A Little About Me!

Hi! I'm Rozita, a UX/UI Designer based in the Bay Area, Menlo Park. My journey has taken me through different creative fields from fashion design and retail to product design. I love problem-solving, making things beautiful yet functional, and creating experiences that truly help people.

Before moving to the U.S. in 2016, I ran my own fashion brand in Tehran. I studied Material Engineering but was always drawn to fashion and interior design. Working in retail taught me a lot about customer behavior and user experience, which led me to UX/UI design. Now, I'm focused on building intuitive, human-centered products that make life easier.
