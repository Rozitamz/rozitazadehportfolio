My Story

Born and raised in Tehran, a city brimming with culture, history, and creativity, I moved to the U.S. in 2016 to explore new opportunities. My journey has taken me through roles in fashion design, sales, and customer service, each contributing to my growth as a creative problem-solver and empathetic collaborator.

My background in fashion design instilled a love for aesthetics, functionality, and detail, while my experience in sales and management taught me to understand user needs deeply and solve diverse challenges. These experiences have shaped my perspective as a UX/UI designer, empowering me to create intuitive and impactful solutions.

Outside of design, I am passionate about staying active, planting, traveling, and hiking. These hobbies fuel my creativity and inspire me to see the world from different perspectives. Empathy and helping others have always been central to my approach, whether in collaboration, teaching, or crafting user-centric designs.

As a UX/UI designer, I aim to turn complex ideas into simple, human-centered products that make a difference in people's lives. I am excited to continue growing and contributing to meaningful projects that improve how we interact with the world.
